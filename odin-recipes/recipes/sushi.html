Sushi Recipe

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish that has become popular worldwide for its delicate flavors, artistic presentation, and diverse ingredients. It typically consists of vinegared rice (known as "shari" or "sumeshi") combined with various ingredients such as raw or cooked seafood, vegetables, and sometimes tropical fruits, wrapped in seaweed (nori) or served as bite-sized pieces.

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Nori
  • Fish
  • Avocado
  • Cucumber
  • Carrot
  • Asparagus
  • Wasabi
  • Soy sauce
  • Pickled Ginger
  • green onions
  • masago

Steps

Rinse and cook short-grain Japanese rice. Season with a mixture of rice vinegar, sugar, and salt.

Lay a sheet of nori on a bamboo rolling mat. Spread sushi rice evenly over the nori, leaving a margin at one edge.

Arrange thinly sliced fish, avocado, cucumber, or other fillings horizontally on the rice

Roll the bamboo mat from the bottom edge, tucking the fillings tightly. Seal the edge with water.

Slice the roll into bite-sized pieces using a sharp knife. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
